The Situation

Strong July baseline (₹47.48L, 2,648 orders) but the brand needed a disciplined system to scale—geo-led acquisition, clean cross-channel signals, and a creative expansion plan without losing efficiency.

What We Ran

P1P2P3–H1H2H3 plus HERO–HUB–HYGIENE: Meta scaled to ₹12.07L spend at 5.3× ROAS in August (₹64L of ₹80L revenue); layered Google, influencers, and WhatsApp. Budget grew to ₹28.21L/month by October—peak ₹1.31Cr revenue and 7,507 orders.
